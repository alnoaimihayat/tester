Introduction:

Hello everyone, welcome to "Through my eyes" website. The goal of it is to express my feelings and thoughts using art and share my techniques and skills in charcoal art to anyone in this community who loves art and aspires to evolve and enhance their skills and that is by building and creating an original book that I called: “How To Be An Artist Using Coal”, where I would be drawing using coal a mixture of themes and types of drawings along with a clear description about it (how did I feel when drawing it, why did I drew it, what techniques I used for it…), these drawings don’t have a specific number limit where I would just keep on drawing until I feel like stopping as well as I would keep updating my drawings as well as any recent projects that will be displayed down the page below.
